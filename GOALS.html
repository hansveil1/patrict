SURVIVE EACH LEVEL

The primary objective is to make it through each level alive, fending off hordes of zombies and reaching the safe room at the end.

RESCUE MISSIONS

Some levels involve rescuing other survivors or securing important resources. Your goal is to locate and assist these individuals while fighting off the infected.

SURVIVAL CHALLENGES

In survival mode, the goal is simply to last as long as possible against endless waves of zombies. The longer you survive, the higher your score.

COMPAIGN COMPLETION

Completing the entire campaign on various difficulty levels is a major goal. This requires teamwork, strategy, and perseverance to overcome increasingly difficult challenges.
