ADANTAGES OF COACH:

*High Health*: Coach has a higher base health compared to other survivors, making him more resilient to damage.

*Melee Expertise*: Coach excels in close-quarters combat, making him effective with melee weapons like the baseball bat and katana.

*Friendly and Supportive*: Coach's positive and encouraging personality can boost team morale and cohesion during intense situations.
















DISADANTAGES OF COACH:

*Slower Speed*: Coach is generally slower than other survivors, which can make it challenging for him to keep up with the team during fast-paced escapes.

*Limited Mobility*: His larger size can sometimes make it harder for Coach to navigate through tight spaces or evade incoming attacks.

*Reliance on Close Combat*: While effective in melee combat, Coach may struggle against long-range threats without appropriate firearms or support from teammates.
