COACH

  • COACH

In Left 4 Dead 2, Coach is one of the four playable survivors. He's a former high school football coach known for his leadership skills and positive attitude. Coach brings a sense of strength and determination to the group as they navigate through the zombie-infested environments. His memorable lines and supportive nature make him a beloved character among players.

FRANCIS

  • FRANCIS

Francis is another character from the original Left 4 Dead game. He's recognizable by his biker appearance, complete with tattoos, leather vest, and sunglasses. Francis is known for his tough and outspoken personality, often expressing disdain for various aspects of the apocalypse, such as the infected, safe houses, and even certain types of firearms. Despite his gruff exterior, he's a valued member of the survivor group, contributing his skills and firepower to help the team survive.

LOUIS

  • LOUIS

Louis is another one of the playable Survivors in Left 4 Dead. He's characterized as a regular guy working in an office before the outbreak. He's often nervous and panicky but still determined to survive. He provides a more relatable perspective compared to the other Survivors.

BILL

  • BILL

Bill is a character from the original Left 4 Dead game. He's a tough Vietnam War veteran and one of the four playable survivors. Bill is known for his grizzled demeanor, leadership qualities, and his memorable lines during gameplay. He often serves as the de facto leader of the group, providing guidance and support to his fellow survivors as they battle hordes of infected.
