ADANTAGES OF LOUIS:

*Ammo Scavenger*: Louis has the ability to find more ammunition than other survivors. This can be crucial during intense firefights where ammunition conservation is key.

*Versatility*: Louis can effectively use a wide range of firearms, providing flexibility in adapting to different combat situations.

*Positive Attitude*: Louis maintains a positive demeanor, which can boost team morale and cohesion during challenging scenarios.
















DISADANTAGES OF LOUIS:

*Lower Health*: Louis typically has lower base health compared to other survivors, making him more vulnerable to damage from infected and special infected.

*No Combat Specialization*: Louis lacks any unique combat abilities or specialties, relying solely on standard weaponry and teamwork to survive.

*Balanced Attributes*: While Louis doesn't have any glaring weaknesses, he also lacks standout strengths compared to some other survivors, requiring players to rely more on strategy and coordination to succeed.
