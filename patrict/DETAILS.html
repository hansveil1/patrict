SURVIVORS

Players control one of four survivors: Bill, Francis, Louis, and Zoey. Each survivor has a distinct personality and background but shares the same goal of escaping the zombie-infested areas.

INFECTED

The primary enemies in the game are the Infected, which include common infected, special infected, and boss infected. Common infected are standard zombies, while special infected have unique abilities such as pouncing, spitting acid, or pulling survivors with a tongue. Boss infected include the Tank, a massive and powerful zombie.

CAMPAIGNS

The game consists of four campaigns, each with multiple levels. The survivors must navigate through different environments, such as urban areas, forests, and industrial complexes, while completing objectives and fending off waves of Infected.

COOPERATIVE GAMEPLAY

Left 4 Dead" emphasizes cooperative gameplay, with up to four players working together to survive. Players must coordinate their actions, share resources, and watch each other's backs to progress through the levels.

AI DIRECTOR

The game features an AI Director system that dynamically adjusts the difficulty based on the players' performance. This ensures that each playthrough feels unique and challenging.

VERSUS MODE

In addition to the cooperative campaign mode, "Left 4 Dead" offers a Versus mode where players can take on the role of Infected and compete against the survivors. This mode adds a competitive element to the gameplay.
